GST Notification No. 10/2023 - Central Tax
Subject: Mandatory E-Invoicing Turnover Above 5 Crore

In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (8) of section 143 of the Central Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017, the Central Government hereby reduces the mandatory e-invoicing threshold limit.
Any business registered under GST having an annual turnover exceeding INR 5 Crore in any financial year from 2017-18 onwards must generate structured e-invoices containing IRNs and QR codes for all B2B transactions.
This amendment is effective starting 1st August 2023. Non-compliance results in invalid invoices and a penalty of ₹10,000 per incorrect entry.
